Bootcamp Prep: 8 Weeks, Part-time

 

Bootcamp Prep is an 8-week part-time course designed to help you achieve three goals:

  1. Learn the fundamentals of coding in JavaScript, HTML, & CSS.
  2. Find out for sure if you truly enjoy coding and show promise as a developer.
  3. Get admitted to a top bootcamp to build on your skills & become a software engineer!

This course is divided up into a series of 4 modules. You have the choice of completing your Core Training in-person or remotely. The remaining modules are only offered remotely.

CORE Training: 4 WEEKS (In-Person or REMOTE)

  • Meet two evenings per week in a classroom with a 5:1 ratio of students to teachers.
  • Work through challenges isolating specific learning objectives in JavaScript, HTML, & CSS.
  • Get live support from instructors during class & office hours.

Guest Lectures & Networking: 1 week (REMOTE)

  • Join two evening lectures focused on intermediate topics with teachers from partner bootcamps.
  • Connect with FSC alumni enrolled in bootcamps you're interested in to get the inside scoop.

FINAL PROJECT: 1 WEEK (REMOTE)

  • Pull together everything you've learned over the course to build the front-end for a web application!
  • Work independently or with a partner from class.

Mock Interview & Advising (Remote)

  • Complete a mock bootcamp interview with an instructor to receive your official Bootcamp Readiness Score & next step recommendations.
